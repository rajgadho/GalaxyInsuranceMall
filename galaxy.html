About us

Meet our insurance agency.

We established in 2008, Galaxy Insurance Mall of Mumbai India, is an Independent Insurance Agency dedicated to providing comprehensive coverage that our customers have come to trust. We have the staff and skills to work for you and your business, limiting your risks and protecting the equity you have built. We can review your present needs and project your future needs, helping you prepare for every contingency. Our employees are specialists in the field of insurance and are dedicated to working for your needs. Our employees do have the knowledge and experience to determine the kind of insurance coverage you require, they also care enough to listen to your needs and concerns. Our customer service department is staffed with individuals who treat you as a friend rather than a customer.

General

As a full service agency we can offer coverage for individuals, families and business.
